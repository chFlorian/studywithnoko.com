Powerful Features

Pomodoro Timer

Focus with the proven 25-minute study sessions followed by short breaks. Your study buddies will guide you through each session.

Add Friends

Connect with friends and study together virtually. See who's studying right now and motivate each other to stay focused.

Leaderboard

Compete with friends on study streaks, total study time, and achievements. Friendly competition makes studying more engaging!

⭐

Earn XP & Streaks

Gain experience points for every study session and maintain your streak. Watch your progress grow as you build consistent study habits.

🏅

Unlock Awards

Collect special awards and badges for your achievements. Show off your progress and see what your friends have earned too!

🎯

Personalized Goals

Set custom study goals and let your study buddies help you achieve them. Track your progress and celebrate milestones together.
